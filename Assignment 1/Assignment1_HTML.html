How to Make a Quesadilla

What you will need..

To make a basic quesadilla you will need to gather the following tools and ingredients:
  • (2) Tortillas
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Stovetop
  • Pan (big enough for a tortilla)
  • Spatula
  • Butter knife
You may use any type of cheese but I have found that a Mexican-style cheese blend tastes the best.

How to prepare your masterpiece...

Quesadillas are quite easy to make. By following these steps you may cook your own piece of heaven.
  1. Place pan on stovetop and heat on medium-low heat.
  2. Take 2 tortillas and lightly butter each on one side.
  3. Place 1 tortilla - butter side down - onto the pan.
  4. Sprinkle cheese onto cooking tortilla.
  5. Place last tortilla - butter side up - onto cheese.
  6. Flip the quesadilla when the bottom has cooked to your liking.
